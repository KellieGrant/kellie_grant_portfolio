About Me

Hey there! I’m Kellie Grant, a front-end developer who loves building clean, responsive websites and learning cool new things along the way.

My journey into tech hasn’t been the most traditional, and that’s part of what makes it exciting. I originally studied Photography & Media Design and spent several years running my own photography business, where I learned the ins and outs of visual storytelling and working with clients.

When the pandemic hit, like a lot of people, I had to pivot. I found work as a driver to keep things moving, and eventually ended up working in the construction industry. It paid the bills, but it never really sparked anything for me.

Then I started learning to code, and something just clicked. What began as casual curiosity quickly turned into one of my favorite things to do. I love the blend of logic, creativity, and endless learning that front-end development brings.

Outside of coding, I’m a drummer, music lover, and a fan of minimal desk setups. I also run a quiet little YouTube channel called CodeLab ASMR, where I combine my love for coding and calm, focused workspaces.

I’m currently looking for opportunities to grow as a developer, contribute to real projects, and connect with others in the tech world. Thanks for stopping by!
